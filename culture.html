Culture Lenggong

Lenggong Valley is rich in cultural heritage that dates back to ancient times. The community here has preserved traditional practices and customs, which are reflected in their daily lives and festivities.

Traditional Practices

The valley is known for its traditional dance and music performances, which are integral to celebrations and ceremonies. Visitors can witness these cultural expressions and gain insight into the history and traditions of the local people.

Cultural Cuisine

The local cuisine is another highlight of Lenggong culture. Traditional dishes, often prepared with locally sourced ingredients, provide a unique culinary experience that reflects the region's heritage.
