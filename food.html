Best Food Spots in Lenggong

Teringat Lenggong

Discover the rich culinary heritage of Lenggong at Restaurant Teringat Lenggong, which is well-known for its talam dishes that tells a story of tradition and taste. For those seeking a divine culinary experience accompanied by the breathtaking views of Tasik Raban, this is the ultimate destination. Indulge in a feast that tantalizes your taste buds while soaking in the serene beauty of the surrounding landscape.click here for more

Restoran Tasik Raban

Located on the right side of the road after the breathtaking views of Raban Lake, Restaurant Tasik Raban offers wide selection of village food that is both tasty and appetizing. Both locals and tourists flock here to enjoy the appetizing dishes, including their well-known Tempoyak and Ikan Pekasam, which will surely elevate your taste buds. Enjoy the self-service convenience and reasonable prices while savoring a delightful meal in a picturesque setting. click here for more

Rumah Makan Rusa Lenggong

Rumah Makan Rusa Lenggong offers a unique dining experience with its exotic and delicious dishes. This restaurant specializes in a variety of deer cuisines such as Sup Daging Rusa, Rusa Masak Kicap, and Daging Bakar Rusa. From their well-crafted menu to their warm services, every visit is sure to leave customers satisfied and eager to return. click here for more

Warung Ikan Bakar Sepuh

Warung Ikan Bakar Sepuh is a must-visit destination for mouthwatering breakfast and lunch dishes. Famous for its delectable Ikan Bakar Sepuh and Ikan Pekasam, this warung offers an authentic taste of local cuisine, often served on talam for a traditional experience. The excellent service and affordable prices make it a popular spot for both locals and tourists. click here for more

Suak Saudagar Espresso

Escape the modern hustle and step into a charming journey back in time at Suak Saudagar Espresso. This coffee shop decorated in a nostalgic kampung style, and nestled near a serene lake, offering breathtaking views as customers sip their coffee. They serve a delightful selection of drinks, scrumptious cakes, and flavorful laksa. click here for more

Pio'kopi

Pio'kopi is a hidden gem located in Kampung Sumpitan, perfect for coffee enthusiasts and dessert lovers alike. At this coffee shop, customers can indulge in a variety of meticulously crafted coffee options, each brewed to perfection and also mouthwatering desserts, perfect for satisfying sweeth tooth. Non-coffee drinks also available here for all customers who didn't drink coffee. click here for more
